Pasta Bolognese

Description

Pasta Bolognese is a traditional Italian dish consisting of delicious pasta with minced beef and tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • tomato sauce
  • spices: pepper, salt and olive oil
  • minced beef

Preparation

  1. Heat pan on medium heat, add oil
  2. add minced beef and fry until it catches color
  3. season meat, and add tomato sauce, lower heat
  4. cook pasta for 11 minutes
  5. when done, mix together and enjoy!
